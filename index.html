About Us

Introduction

KN Home Trading is a hardware store operating in Kemaman, Terengganu. This store is located in Kemaman, Terengganu and provides various types of products such as building materials, glass, paint, carpentry tools and basic hardware items for individual use, contractors, and local communities. KN Home Trading is also identified as a registered hardware store involved in the "retail sale of hardware, paints and glass" category, which is the retail sale of hardware, paint, glass, and related equipment.
